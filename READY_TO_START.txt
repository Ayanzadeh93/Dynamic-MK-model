What Will Happen:
-----------------
1. Training will start with 10-fold cross validation
2. For each fold (~41 images validation):
   - Train SqueezeNet for up to 50 epochs
   - Show progress bars with loss/accuracy
   - Save best model when validation improves
   - Apply early stopping if no improvement

3. After all folds complete:
   - Generate comprehensive metrics
   - Create beautiful visualizations
   - Save all results

Expected Time:
--------------
With your GPU: ~1.5-2 hours for 10 folds
(About 10-12 minutes per fold)

You can monitor progress in real-time!
